About My Blog

I love blogs. I love reading blogs. I love reading all about all of the experiences that people have and can relate too. It makes me feel less crazy for having the experiences I have.

I have the privilege of sharing my life's experiences with several peers and young mentoress. for the the last three years those people have been asking me to start my own blog filled with all of those experiences.

You will see that I am a pretty candid person about myself. Transparency is something that keeps me going. That is why I love blogs; it's a way for people to be real. So I have given in and with the help of my super computer-genius husband, Matthew, I am creating this blog for all of us. I intend to share stories about my history of addictions, my healing and faith, my marriage journey, the ups and downs of becoming a mother of two beautiful kids and more.

It's called Full Disclosure because I intend to do just that; fully disclosure everything I have experienced. My life is both messy and wonderful. God has brought me from some very dark places and into some amazing blessings. Although I am sure to make some people offended or uncomfortable with some by sharing some of my experiences, I believe this blog will offer healing and freedom to some of you as well as myself.
